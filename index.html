About info for the iLit4GEE-AI web app developed by GeoAIR Lab

This is the accompany interactive web app for the review paper (invited feature paper), titled "Google Earth Engine and Artificial Intelligence (AI): A Comprehensive Review", which was published in the special issue (The Future of Remote Sensing: Harnessing the Data Revolution) of the open access journal Remote Sensing.

    New Data Entry Form for iLit4GEE-AI Web App

    Are you the authors of, or are you aware of, any published paper(s) in GEE + AI that are not included in this web app? If so, please submit the data for the paper through this data entry form. Through this form, GEE+ AI scholars/practitioners can submit the data for recent publications and/or papers published in the past that our lab missed in our review (https://doi.org/10.3390/rs14143253). GeoAIR lab members will review the data submitted to our online data entry form and determine whether it should be included into our web application. Our ultimate goal for this web app is to build an up-to-date, searchable literature list for the GEE and AI community in order to advance science and to support researchers to tackle challenging problems such as global warming. Thank you for helping build an open-source data catalog for all GEE+AI literature!

    If you need a detailed explanation of data fields while you are working on and submitting this online form for your GEE and AI paper, please check our data explanation web page. If you still have questions, feel free to contact the director of the GeoAIR Lab Dr. Liping Yang (lipingyang@unm.edu).
